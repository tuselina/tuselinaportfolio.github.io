Selina's Folio

Digital Design Project - Interaction




IDEA
Inspired by the earthy woody tones of the forest and nature. The nudes, beige, dark browns and silvers compliment each other. With an overall warm toned colour palette.
Cooking is a very grounded and practical skill. This theme contains basic everyday cooking utensils that households use. It creates a very homey, relatable and comfortable atmosphere.
When I searched up cooking utensil icons online I could only see black icons that have a very flat 2D design with no depth. I wanted to create a flowing, feminine and natural design.
SKETCHES
I researched different cooking utensils and roughly sketched out designs that I liked. I picked the more simple but detailed versions. Those that have equal proportion in wood handle and metal end. I kept them simple and easily identifiable. I copied the rough outline of the sketches using organic forms rather than geometric.
INTERACTIONS
The sound effects I used for each utensil would represent the sound it would emulate when used. Visually when the sound is emitted the utensil is being used with food such as steak meat, dough, crumbs, soup and lettuce.
The interactions and animations I implemented in my design are
as the mouse hovers over the icons,
wooden spoon it spins clockwise as if it is falling and the sound effect plays of the spoon dropping on the table.
The ladle spins clockwise and scoops up soup; sound effect of metal ladle mixing in a pot.
Scissors qbout to cut a string of pasta; Snip sound effect of scissors closing.
Pizza cutter rolling gainst cardboard and in the visuals pieces of dough crumbs can be seen under the utensil.
Steak stick punctures meat and creates a squishy meat sound being poked.
Whisk tilts diagonally and mixes dough mixture.
Tongs clinking into each other and closing inwards whilst picking up a piece of salad lettuce.
Knife sharpening sound effect and visuals are cutting a steak.
Spatula scraping the pan as it scoops up pancake dough.
